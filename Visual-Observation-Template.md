For each page of each book, observe and record:

1. **Is the image visible?**

   - ⚠️ Visible but with issues
   - ❌ Not visible

2. **Visual quality (if visible):**

   - Acceptable (visible but somewhat pixelated/blurry)
   - Poor (very pixelated, visible artifacts)
   - Unacceptable (barely readable)

3. **Observed problems:**
   - Image cropped
   - Incorrect colors
   - Excessive pixelation
   - Compression artifacts
   - Distorted image
   - Other (specify)
